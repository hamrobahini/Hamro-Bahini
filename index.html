Prakriti Dhakal

Prakriti Dhakal, a youth leader and a public health aspirant, an initiator, supporter, and participant of numerous leadership projects and a member of esteemed national and international organizations, is a recipient of distinguished awards and honors and believes in community mobilization for a healthy and happy community.

About Me

Prakriti Dhakal is a 25 years young, energetic youth leader striving to positively impact community health. The public health aspirant led the first Nepal Health Conclave in 2021 which brought together stakeholders from Nepal and around the world to discuss the confronted challenges, emerging opportunities, and ways forward to strengthen the health system of Nepal. She is pursuing her graduate degree in Non-Profit MBA at King’s College Nepal. She is currently working as a program officer at Pokhara Research Centre and has been coordinating Nepal’s Pre-Professionals Parliamentary Support Program in Gandaki and Karnali Province. The former US Embassy Youth Council member and Community Engagement Exchange Fellow 2020/21, IREX USA, is also an Assistant Secretary at Rotary International District 3292, Nepal and Bhutan. She has attended esteemed training, conventions, and seminars regarding her field of interest like WHO simulations at UNSW Australia 2018, Rotary International Conventions in Germany 2019, and Rotary International Convention in Houston 2022, and has also been to the World Health Organization in Geneva, Switzerland. Ms. Dhakal has been honored with distinguished awards and titles for her exceptional endeavors and achievements. She is a recipient of the Tunza World Hidden Eco-Hero award 2016, Ms. Rotassia 2020, Best Ambassador 2017 by Tunza Eco Generation, and Best Mentor of 2015 by Environment Friendly Local Governance Program (EFLGP) along with other honors and rewards. Ms. Dhakal envisions a community where health literacy, health services, and health infrastructure is adequately available, accessible and affordable to all.
